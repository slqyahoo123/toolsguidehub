## üîç The 3 Meta Tags You Can't Afford to Ignore

Let's cut through the noise. Out of dozens of meta tag options, these three drive 90% of your SEO results:

### 1. Title Tag (The King)
The title tag is your first impression in search results. Google rewrites bad ones, which kills your CTR.

**Anatomy of a Perfect Title:**
```html
<title>How to [Solution] | [Brand]</title>
```

**Example:**
*   **Bad:** "Homepage - Welcome"
*   **Good:** "AI Writing Tools: 15 Best Options for 2026 | ToolsHub"

**Why it works:**
*   Keyword at the front ("AI Writing Tools")
*   Number creates curiosity ("15 Best")
*   Year signals freshness ("2026")
*   Brand trust (" | ToolsHub")

---

### 2. Meta Description (The Sales Pitch)
Think of this as your Google Ads copy‚Äîexcept it's free.

**Formula:**
```
[Problem] ‚Üí [Solution] ‚Üí [Benefit/CTA]
```

**Example:**
> "Struggling to write engaging content? Discover 15 AI tools that cut writing time by 70%. Compare features and pricing. Free trial links included."

**Breakdown:**
1.  **Hook:** "Struggling to write..."
2.  **Promise:** "cut writing time by 70%"
3.  **Social proof:** "15 AI tools"
4.  **CTA:** "Compare features..."

---

### 3. Open Graph Image (The Social Magnet)
When someone shares your link on LinkedIn or Facebook, this is the image that appears.

**Specs:**
*   **Size:** 1200 x 630 pixels
*   **Format:** JPG or PNG
*   **File size:** Under 1MB

**What to include:**
*   Your headline in large, bold text
*   Your logo
*   A relevant visual

### Do keywords in meta descriptions help SEO?
Not directly for rankings, but yes for CTR. When someone searches "AI writing tools 2026," and your description includes that exact phrase, it gets **bolded** in search results, catching the eye.

### How often should I update meta tags?
*   **Annually:** Refresh year-specific tags (e.g., "2025" ‚Üí "2026")
*   **Quarterly:** Test new CTAs or keyword variations
*   **Immediately:** If CTR is below 2% after 30 days
