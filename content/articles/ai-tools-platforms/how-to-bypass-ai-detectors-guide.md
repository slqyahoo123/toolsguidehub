## üîç How Do AI Detectors Work?

To beat the detector, you must understand how it thinks. Detectors like GPTZero, Turnitin, and Originality.ai primarily look for two things:

### 1. Perplexity (Complexity)
**Perplexity** measures how "surprised" a model is by your text. 
*   **Low Perplexity:** The text is predictable (like "The cat sat on the mat"). AI loves this.
*   **High Perplexity:** The text is creative, chaotic, or uses unusual word choices. Humans write like this.

### 2. Burstiness (Sentence Variation)
**Burstiness** measures the variation in sentence structure.
*   **AI Writing:** Tends to be monotonous. Medium sentence. Medium sentence. Medium sentence.
*   **Human Writing:** Is spiky! We write a very long, complex sentence that winds its way through an idea... and then we stop. Short sentence. Boom.

## üõ†Ô∏è 5 Proven Techniques to Humanize AI Text

Here is the exact workflow we use to take a raw ChatGPT draft and make it undetectable.

### Strategy 1: The "Grandma Test" (Add Anecdotes)
AI cannot have a childhood memory. 
*   **AI:** "Walking is good for health."
*   **Human:** "I remember my grandmother walking 5 miles every day until she was 90."
*   **Action:** Inject one personal story or "I" statement every 300 words.

### Strategy 2: Break the Patterns
If you see three sentences in a row that start with "Additionally," "Furthermore," or "Moreover"‚Äîdelete them. These are dead giveaways of AI structure.

### Strategy 3: Use Specific Data
AI often hallucinates or speaks in generalities. Humans use specific numbers.
*   **AI:** "Many people use smartphones."
*   **Human:** "In 2025, 6.8 billion people used smartphones daily."
